Make this bag by hand!

Get Started!

In 5 easy steps you can make this handy shopping bag!

Wrong sides together sew the edges of the fabric together leaving a 1cm seam allowance. Turn the handle right side out and iron flat. Apply the interfacing to the bag handle fabric (optional). Wrong sides together sew the edges of the fabric together leaving a 1cm seam allowance. Turn the handle right side out and iron flat. Apply the interfacing to the bag handle fabric (optional).
